About Us

There are hundreds of stray dogs in the Houston area. Many rescue shelters are already fighting to give loving homes and second chances to as many dogs as they can. However, most often the ones that don't even get a second glance are the older dogs and "agressive" breeds such as pit bulls and rottweilers to name a few. Little do people know these are the most loyal and loving towards all people.There is no such thing as an aggressive breed, just misunderstood dogs. Here at Save a Bully, our goal is to raise awareness about the misconceptions about dog breeds. Its not the dog that is agressive but lack of training and knowledge about dog's wants and needs that leads to unfortunate events.
