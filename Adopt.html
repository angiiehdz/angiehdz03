How it Works

Step One: Get Informed

Before you can apply and meet the pups, you must first read some important documents that can be found in our facility or email us to send pdf versions. We are so excited for you to bring home a new member of the family but their health and safety is our top priority. We must make sure you are properly informed about the responsibilities and preparations needed to take home a dog.

Step Two:Fill out Application

The next step is for you to fill out an application online or in person. We will ask some questions about your family lifestyle and what you're looking for in your new best friend. These questions help us see which dog or dogs are the best match for you.

Step Three:Meet Your New Best Friend!

After our review you get the opportunity to meet the bullies and see who you hit it off with at the center. Once you've picked your new best friend, you will fill out final paperwork that includes adoption fee, aggreement to spa/neuter, microchip, and surrender. Once all that is done you're ready to make everlasting memories with your new best friend!
